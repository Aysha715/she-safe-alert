Safety Tips

Trust Your Instincts
If something feels wrong, it probably is. Leave the situation immediately or move to a crowded place.
Share Your Location
Enable live location sharing with trusted contacts on WhatsApp or Google Maps before traveling.
Avoid Isolation
Stick to well-lit, busy streets. Avoid taking shortcuts through dark alleys or parks at night even if it saves time.
Emergency Numbers
Keep local emergency numbers (Police, Ambulance, Women Helpline) on speed dial.
